Ruthenium

Ruthenium is a transition metal; its name derives from the Latin name Ruthenia which refers to the Rutenia region, now called Ukraine. Ruthenium together with rhodium, palladium, osmium, iridium and platinum composes the elements known as the metals of the platinum group. It is a hard and white metal, which together with platinum and palladium can form very hard alloys widely used in jewelry and metal/porcelain dental prostheses.
