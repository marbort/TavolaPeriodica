Carbon

Carbon (C) is a non metal that can be found in nature in its original state in two different crystalline forms: graphite, one of the softest materials, and diamond, one of the hardest known in nature. It can be found in a minimal percentage in the earth’s crust and in particular in carbonates, petroleum, carbon and natural gas. It can be find in the atmosphere too, mostly in the form of carbon dioxide (CO2), highly important for plant growth, even though the highest percentage of carbon can be found, as a constituent, in the basic structure of all living organisms. It can be used in industries as primary material or as reinforcement fiber in the production of sport amenities, alloys and plastics. In order to represent Carbon i wore black clothes and a plastic skirt, on top of which I applied pencils and diamond patches. I am holding a tennis racket in one hand and i’m wearing a band with a representation of a chain of carbon atoms drawn on top. I chose to have a natural, green background with a bush as carbon represents the tree of life.
