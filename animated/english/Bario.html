Barium

Barium (Ba) is an element of the second group and an alkaline-earth metal, solid in ambient air temperature and found in the looks of white salt in Barium sulphate. This salt is often used in healthcare, as it is given to patients before taking an x ray of the gastrointestinal tract: it is fluorescent when struck by x rays.. For this same reason, we have taken a gastrointestinal tract x ray and used it to represent the most common use of a Ba compound. Our picture represents a patient between two radiologists observing the results of their x ray.
