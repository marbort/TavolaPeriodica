Helium

The second element of the periodic table, with its atomic mass of 4.002602 u it is seven times less heavy than air (average dry mass 28.96 u). This characteristic lightness is responsible for lifting the balloons filled with it into the air, making all children look up to the sky amazed to see their balloon fly away higher and higher towards the sun. And it is from the Greek name of our star that the name of this element, whose inhalation makes all voice cheerful, playful and subtle as a child's, comes from.
