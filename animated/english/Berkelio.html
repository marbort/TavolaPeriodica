Berkelium

Berkelium is a radioactive metal element that can be obtained by bombarding Americium with alpha particles. Berkelium atomic number is 97, its symbol is Bk and its name derives from the city of Berkeley, where it was first isolated in 1949 at University of California. This element is extremely rare in nature, it is a radioactive metal belonging to the actinide family. Because of its rarity, there are still no practical uses of this metal.
