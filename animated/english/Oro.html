Gold

Gold is a soft, heavy, ductile and malleable metal, it belongs to the transition metals of the 6th period; it is yellow, unless it is in the form of nanoparticles, where it takes on completely different colors. It is an excellent conductor and its density is among the higher found in metals (1930 kg / m 3). It is a non-reactive metal, in fact it is found pure in the rocks in the form of nuggets. It reacts with mercury forming an amalgam. Its symbol Au derives from the Latin aurum .
