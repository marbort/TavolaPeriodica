Palladium

Palladium is a rare metal, silvery-white in appearance, belonging to the group of platinum, which also chemically resembles to it. It is mainly extracted from certain minerals of copper and nickel. The picture shows the operation of a catalytic converter, whose internal catalyst is also made of palladium. The polluting compounds that enter as nitrogen oxides (NOx), carbon monoxide (CO) and unburned hydrocarbons (HC) are transformed into harmless compounds, such as water, carbon dioxide and nitrogen.
